01) Write a simple PL/SQL program  to calculate the perimeter of a circle with radius=5 cm (Note: Take pi value as 3.14)

DECLARE
    radius NUMBER := 5;
    pi CONSTANT NUMBER := 3.14;
    perimeter NUMBER;
BEGIN
    perimeter := 2 * pi * radius;
    
    DBMS_OUTPUT.PUT_LINE('The perimeter of the circle with radius ' || radius || ' cm is: ' || perimeter || ' cm');
END;
